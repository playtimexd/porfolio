Nova is a node canvas for game art and previs — chain prompts, images, video and 3D, block real camera shots in a 3D stage, or brief the agent and watch it generate straight onto your board. Built for production at Behaviour.

＋ New project Open Nova
🧩
Node canvas Wire Prompt → Image → Video, plus 3D and upload nodes. Typed connectors, up to 9 references per node, and live previews right inside each node.
✳
Agent art director Describe what you want, attach references, and the agent generates straight onto your canvas — in the ratio you ask for, no wiring required.
🎬
3D Director previs Open a full 3D stage — pose characters, equip props, frame the camera — then capture a shot into your project, ready to feed any model.
🖼
Reference-driven Drop or wire in reference images (and video on the video model), then generate a new shot conditioned on all of them. Edit results in-place with the built-in image editor.
🚀
Multi-model Nano Banana, Gemini, GPT Image / Sora, Seedance and Meshy — one canvas, swap the model per node. Bring your own keys.
